Module 08 • Digital Intelligence

Measuring Brand Health

Session Focus

Translating intangible brand perceptions into measurable digital indicators to evaluate performance, detect risks, and guide strategic PR decisions.

1. Brand Equity Indicators

How do we quantify trust and emotional connection? We look at digital behaviors:

Branded Search

Volume of intent

Direct Traffic

Brand recall

Repeat Engagement

Loyalty depth

Advocacy Rate

UGC & Shares

2. Sentiment & Share of Voice

Sentiment Analysis categorizes the emotional tone of brand mentions.

Strategic Insight: Share of Voice (SOV)

Formula: Brand Mentions ÷ Total Category Mentions

A growing SOV combined with positive sentiment indicates strong brand momentum.

3. Brand Lift & Listening

Tools like Brandwatch and Google Trends allow us to track awareness and favorability in real-time.

  • Brand Awareness: Tracking top-of-mind recall.
  • Social Listening: Detecting emerging crises before they scale.
  • Competitor Benchmarking: Comparing geographic demand patterns.

Activity: The Sentiment Detective (20 mins)

Challenge: Analyze a controversy feed and calculate the Net Sentiment Score.

((Positive - Negative) / Total Mentions) x 100

Outcome: Propose one PR action (Apology, Clarification, or Pause in Paid Media) based on the data.

Key Takeaways

  • Brand health must be measured, not guessed.
  • Sentiment is an early warning system for reputational risk.
  • Data supports strategy—it does not replace human judgment.
