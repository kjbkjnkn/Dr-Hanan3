Module 06 • Mid-Course Assignment

Practical Workshop 1: Legacy Rebranding

Workshop Purpose

Challenge: Apply brand strategy, identity, and content principles to digitally transform a struggling legacy brand for a Gen Z (social-first) audience.

The Target: Gen Z

Key behaviors to address in your strategy:

  • Mobile-first & Social-first behavior.
  • Preference for authenticity over perfection.
  • Strong value alignment (community, transparency).
  • Primary usage: TikTok, Instagram, YouTube.

Strategy Tasks

Task 1

New Positioning

Define target audience, core promise, and 3–5 personality traits.

"Why should Gen Z care?"
Task 2

Social-First Persona

Humanize the brand. Define tone, archetype, and platform focus.

"Be a creator, not an advertiser."
Task 3

Launch Idea

Sketch a shareable campaign theme and hashtag strategy.

"Is it shareable and relevant?"

Final Output: 3-Slide Pitch

Submission Format: 3–5 Minute Pitch

Evaluation Criteria

Groups will be assessed on Strategic Clarity, Relevance to Gen Z, Insight-driven Creativity, and Presentation Quality.
